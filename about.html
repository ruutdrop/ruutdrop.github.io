Our Ruut so Far

The genesis of RuutDrop was a frustrating wait over a long weekend for a package to arrive. We set out to solve low-cost, on-demand, inter-state delivery, and quickly realized the tremendous challenges in the last-mile delivery space. Today we are solving the last-mile delivery puzzle.

We are

A group of highly motivated technologists, with years of experience in software development, electronic hardware development, mobile applications, and technology marketing. Many of us hail from Fortune 500 companies like Microsoft, Amazon, Google, and Xerox. Our advisors are senior managers at Fortune 500 companies and CS faculty members, who bring a wealth of experience to guide us through our startup phase.
